# Titanic (1997 Film)

**Directed by:** James Cameron  
**Starring:** Leonardo DiCaprio, Kate Winslet  
**Genre:** Romance, Drama  
**Release Date:** December 19, 1997

---

##  Plot Summary

> *"Nothing on Earth could come between them."*  
A rich girl and a poor artist fall in love aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic.

---

##  Main Characters

- **Jack Dawson** – A poor artist  
- **Rose DeWitt Bukater** – A wealthy young woman  
- **Cal Hockley** – Rose’s fiancé  
- **Molly Brown** – A kind-hearted first-class passenger
